Hi, I'm Aleksa — a programmer by day and an artist when inspiration strikes. I'm from Serbia, and I created this portfolio to preserve and share my artwork as it grows. My style is influenced by Impressionism and its modern echoes. I’m drawn to vivid, colorful interpretations of the world — often painting while listening to audiobooks, lost in stories and brushstrokes at the same time. I love fantasy themes, portraits, and surrealism — not for realism itself, but for the ideas and emotions they evoke. This space is a home for the visual side of me. Thanks for visiting.
